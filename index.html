TapSplit

No internet?
No problem.

TapSplit uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity to share sessions and expenses directly between nearby iPhones — over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct, no server in the middle.

  • 📡
    Works fully offline Nearby sharing over Bluetooth & Wi-Fi Direct
  • ⚡
    Real-time session sync Every member sees expense updates instantly
  • 🧮
    Smart settlement engine Minimises transactions needed to settle all debts
Settle in one tap The app calculates who owes who — and minimises the steps.
